ABOUT ME

My name is Sedye Roozbeh Razavi Rohani. I got my B.Sc. Shiraz University, Shiraz, Iran and my M.Sc. degrees at Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. I was previously a researcher at Machine Learning Laboratory (MLL) in computer engineering department under the supervision of Prof. Mahdieh Soleymani. I am mainly interested in Deep Learning, Sequential Decision Making problems and approaches that pave the way for Artificial General Intelligence. More specifically, I recently studied how inductive basis originated from neuroscientific observation could be injected into Meta Reinforcement Learning methods to make the best of them. I am also excited about other perspectives to solve Sequential Decision-Making problems, say Control Theory and Algorithmic Game Theory.
